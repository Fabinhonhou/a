Our Story

Founded in 1987 by Antonio Santoro, our restaurant has been serving authentic Italian cuisine for over three decades. What started as a small family trattoria has grown into one of the most beloved Italian dining establishments in the city.

At Santoro's, we believe in preserving the traditional flavors of Italy while incorporating modern culinary techniques. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring that each dish tells a story of Italian heritage.

We source only the finest ingredients, many imported directly from Italy, to create dishes that transport you to the streets of Rome, the countryside of Tuscany, or the coast of Sicily with every bite.

"Cooking is an art, but all art requires knowing something about the techniques and materials"
